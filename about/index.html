We Make Pet Products.
That's All We Do. And We Do It Well.

JRK Sourcing is a China-based pet products manufacturing partner. We help brands worldwide — from startup founders to established retailers — design, source, and produce quality pet products.

Our Story

JRK Sourcing was founded by Charlize, who spent years working with Chinese factories across multiple industries before realizing one simple truth: general sourcing agents spread themselves too thin to deliver real quality.

The pet industry was different. US and European brands wanted factory-direct pricing, but most sourcing agents treated pet products as just another line item. They didn't understand that a martingale collar needs different hardware than a flat buckle collar. That a dog bed's foam density matters more than its fabric color.

So Charlize built JRK Sourcing around a single focus: pet products only.

Today, we work with a vetted network of specialized factories across China — each one focused on a specific pet category. Our clients include Kickstarter startups launching their first product, Amazon FBA sellers scaling their brand, and established pet retailers expanding their private label lines.

Our Approach

🔍 Factory-First Sourcing

We don't "find factories" for each order. We maintain long-term relationships with 200+ specialized pet product factories. When you come to us with a design, we already know which factory is the right fit.

📋 Quality Control Built In

Every order goes through our 3-stage QC:

  1. Pre-production — Material and hardware approval
  2. During production — Inline inspection at 50% completion
  3. Pre-shipment — Random sampling per AQL 2.5 standard

🚢 End-to-End Logistics

From sample delivery to full container load — we handle shipping, customs documentation, and delivery to your door or Amazon FBA warehouse.
