Dog Apparel & Accessories

Raincoats, sweaters, pajamas, bandanas, and utility vests. Custom patterns, prints, and branding. XXS to XXL.

Types We Make

🌧️ Raincoats & Waterproof Apparel

Waterproof/breathable outerwear using PVC-free TPU laminated polyester. Reflective piping for night safety. Hood with elastic drawstring. Leg strap loops. Harness access slot on back. Sizes XXS (2lb) to XXL (150lb). Solid colors, camo, tartan, transparent, or custom print.

🧶 Sweaters & Knitwear

Warm, cozy, photo-ready. Polar fleece, anti-pill microfleece, cotton knit, or acrylic/wool blend chunky knits. Cable knit, rib knit, Fair Isle patterns. Matching sweater + bandana sets. Button/clasp closure or pullover style.

😴 Pajamas & Comfort Wear

Cotton jersey, fleece footie PJs, recovery suits (post-surgery), and calming wraps. Supima cotton or bamboo fabric options. Full-body or half-body styles. Ideal for bedtime, recovery, or anxiety relief.

🎀 Bandanas & Neckwear

Simple add-on that boosts perceived value. Reversible designs (2 looks in 1). Triangle fold, pre-tied, or snap-on styles. Seasonal patterns. Custom logo print. Matching human bandana for owner-dog sets. Bulk packs for retailers.

🦺 Safety & Utility Vests

Harness-compatible safety vests. Reflective hunting/outdoor vests. Cooling vests with evaporative cooling fabric. Life jackets with buoyant foam and handle. Service dog / emotional support vests.
