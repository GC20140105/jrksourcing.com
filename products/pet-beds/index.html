Custom Pet Beds & Mats

Any shape, any size, any fabric. Orthopedic, donut, cooling, cave, and travel beds. Fully customizable with your branding.

Types We Make

🛏️ Donut / Nest Beds

Round beds with raised bolsters — pets feel secure and snug. Faux fur, sherpa, microfiber, or cotton covers. Removable/washable covers with zipper closure. Sizes S (20") to XXL (44").

🧊 Cooling Beds

Elevated or gel-based cooling for hot climates. Breathable mesh fabric (PVC mesh or 3D spacer mesh). Steel or aluminum raised frames. Collapsible for travel. Indoor and outdoor use.

🏋️ Orthopedic Beds

For senior dogs, large breeds, and post-surgery recovery. CertiPUR memory foam base. Egg-crate or solid foam options. Waterproof inner liner. Anti-slip bottom (TPR coating).

🏠 Cave / Hooded Beds

Enclosed beds for anxious pets who like to burrow. Soft plush outer, fleece inner. Self-warming reflective lining. Machine washable.

🧳 Travel & Crate Mats

Thin, foldable mats for crates, carriers, or car use. Lightweight and portable. Variety of fabrics. Custom sizes to fit any crate.
