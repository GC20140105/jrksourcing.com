Custom Pet Beds & Mats

Any shape, any size, any fabric. Orthopedic, donut, cooling, cave, and travel beds. Fully customizable with your branding.

Our Product Range

Bestseller

Orthopedic Memory Foam Beds

CertiPUR memory foam base with egg-crate or solid foam options. Waterproof inner liner with anti-slip TPR bottom. Ideal for senior dogs, large breeds, and post-surgery recovery.

Popular

Donut / Cave Beds

Round beds with raised bolsters — pets feel secure and snug. Faux fur, sherpa, microfiber, or cotton covers. Removable/washable covers with zipper closure. Self-warming reflective lining for cave styles.

New

Cooling & Travel Beds

Elevated or gel-based cooling for hot climates. Breathable mesh fabric (PVC mesh or 3D spacer mesh). Steel or aluminum raised frames. Collapsible for travel. Indoor and outdoor use.
