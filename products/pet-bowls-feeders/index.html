Pet Bowls & Feeders

FDA-grade stainless, ceramic, melamine, and slow feeder designs with elevated stands, non-skid mats, and full branding.

Types We Make

🥣 Stainless Steel Bowls

The industry standard — durable, hygienic, easy to clean. 304 food-grade stainless (18/8). Mirror polish or brushed finish. Double-wall insulated options. Non-skid silicone base. Sizes 8oz to 96oz. Custom laser engraving.

🏺 Ceramic Bowls

Premium look with heavy base to prevent tipping. Lead-free, BPA-free food-grade ceramic. Matte or gloss glaze. Custom shapes: round, oval, wide, raised. Hand-painted or decal transfer patterns. Microwave and dishwasher safe.

🪣 Melamine / Plastic Bowls

Lightweight, durable, affordable. FDA-grade melamine. Slow feeder raised patterns. Spill-resistant rim design. Bright colors and patterns. Commercial grade available.

🧩 Slow Feeder / Puzzle Bowls

For dogs that eat too fast — reduces bloat risk. Internal labyrinth patterns at different difficulty levels. Silicone or melamine. Suction cup base. Dishwasher safe. Travel versions with lids.

📏 Elevated & Tilted Stands

Reduces neck strain, improves digestion. Bamboo, teak wood, powder-coated metal, or molded plastic. Adjustable height for growing puppies. Anti-slip feet. Single and double bowl configurations.

🧻 Non-Skid Feeding Mats

Catches spills and protects floors. Silicone or TPR. Raised edges keep water in. Custom shapes: rectangle, oval, bone-shaped. Easy clean — rinse or dishwasher.

Materials Certifications

  • FDA — Food contact safe for US market
  • LFGB — Food contact safe for EU market
  • SGS — Third-party tested for heavy metals & BPA
  • CPSIA — Children's product safety (also covers pet bowls)
