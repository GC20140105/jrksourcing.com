Pet Bowls & Feeders

FDA-grade stainless, ceramic, melamine, and slow feeder designs with elevated stands, non-skid mats, and full branding.

Types We Make

Bestseller

Stainless Steel Bowls

The industry standard — durable, hygienic, easy to clean. 304 food-grade stainless (18/8). Mirror polish or brushed finish. Double-wall insulated options. Non-skid silicone base. Sizes 8oz to 96oz. Custom laser engraving.

Popular

Ceramic & Slow Feeder

Premium look with heavy base to prevent tipping. Lead-free, BPA-free food-grade ceramic. Matte or gloss glaze. Custom shapes. Slow feeder internal labyrinth patterns reduce bloat risk. Silicone or melamine options. Dishwasher safe.

New

Elevated Stands & Sets

Reduces neck strain, improves digestion. Bamboo, teak wood, powder-coated metal, or molded plastic. Adjustable height for growing puppies. Anti-slip feet. Single and double bowl configurations. Non-skid feeding mats also available.
